hand painted frames

Indigo Brown Fine Art Framing specifically design frames for Artists who want bespoke hand finished frames made from natural bare wood and finished using any chosen colour, natural wax, lacquers or gilding.

All our frames are made this way and we do not make or supply pre finished mouldings.

We mostly frame for working artists and not the general public.

natural and untreated

Indigo Brown Fine Art Framing was primarily set up for the purpose of offering a service to the growing number of Artists in Pembrokeshire and the surrounding areas.

It is a pleasure to be able to work closely with our artists and local galleries and to be part of the art scene here in Pembrokeshire
